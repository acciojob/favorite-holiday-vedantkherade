Paris


Paris, the City of Light, is known for its iconic Eiffel Tower, charming cafés, and romantic streets. It blends rich history, art, and fashion into a timeless urban beauty.

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most iconic symbol of Paris offering breathtaking city views.
  2. Louvre Museum – The world's largest art museum, home to masterpieces like the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A historic Gothic cathedral with beautiful architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées & Arc de Triomphe – A grand avenue leading to a monumental arch.
  5. Sacré-Cœur Basilica – A stunning basilica on Montmartre Hill with panoramic views of Paris.
  • Baguette – Freshly baked French bread with a crispy crust.
  • Croissant – A buttery and flaky pastry perfect for breakfast.
  • Escargots – Snails cooked in garlic and parsley butter.
  • Crème Brûlée – A creamy dessert with a caramelized sugar top.
  • Macarons – Colorful almond meringue cookies with delicious filling.
