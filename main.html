Paris


Paris, the capital of France, is known for its iconic landmarks, rich history, art, culture, and romantic atmosphere.

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most iconic landmark of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the famous Mona Lisa painting.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Arc de Triomphe – Built to honor French soldiers.
  5. Sacré-Cœur Basilica – Located on Montmartre Hill with stunning views.
  • Baguette – A crispy French bread.
  • Croissant – A flaky, buttery pastry.
  • Escargots – Snails cooked with garlic butter.
  • Crème Brûlée – A rich custard dessert.
  • Macarons – Colorful almond meringue cookies.
